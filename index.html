About

An engineer at heart, Cam loves to tear things apart to see how they work. If you can somehow catch him when he's not involved in a tech project or studying, he'll likely be at Papa'a beach throwing a frisbee to his exhuberant mutt, Kepler, alongside his husband Emory. Cam was born in California, shaped by New York, and now resides on Kauai.
